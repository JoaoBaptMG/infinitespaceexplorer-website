The game

With smooth motion controls, several challenges and beautiful graphics, Infinite Space Explorer is not the infinite runner you were expecting. Travel trough space while avoiding the hazards that may get in your way and collecting helpful powerups, and try to get the greatest score possible!

About Us

JOÃO BAPTISTA/software and business
I have always been fascinated with games and their complexity, and I always wanted to know how they worked. When I was 9, the Internet presented me with RPG Maker, which was my first tool to make games. However, I discovered a passion for programming and I decided to join this passion with my always-standing dream of making games.

LUCAS RESENDE/graphics and website
Just trying to be a designer, a programmer and a mathematician, not in order. I joined the group as a hobby and my mainly function was to delay the project where and when I can, by not sending sprites in time and saying "almost done" when I have neither started. But i swear, it is not my fault if the game delayed one year.

MARCELO HIPÓLITO/music and sound
I have always been in contact with music, but I never thought I would start composing tracks for games. It all began when I posted a query on a gaming forum in the internet. I started chatting with a programmer who is also a musician, and he introduced me to the market of music composition for games. Since then, I have combined the love I already had for games with the love for music.
